Your First Guitar Chords

Welcome

Learning guitar doesn’t have to be confusing—this page is designed to walk you through the essentials step by step. You’ll start by understanding how to read a chord chart, so you know exactly where to place your fingers on the fretboard. From there, we’ll explore the three core chord types every beginner should know: major, minor, and dominant 7th shapes, all in the open position. Once you’re comfortable forming these chords, you’ll learn how to put them into practice by playing a simple 12-bar blues using a basic, easy-to-follow strumming pattern.

  1. Learn to read chord charts
  2. Learn Major Chords
  3. Learn Minor Chords
  4. Learn 7th Chords
  5. Learn to play the 12 Bar Blues
